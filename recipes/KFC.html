KFC - Kentucky Fried Chicken

Welcome to the KFC recipe page! Here, you'll find a delightful recipe that will make your taste buds dance with joy.

Introducing our treasured family recipe for authentic Kentucky Fried Chicken! Crispy, golden-brown chicken pieces seasoned with a secret blend of herbs and spices, fried to perfection. Each bite is a burst of flavor that transports you to the heart of Southern cuisine.

Ingredients for KFC!

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for heat)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions to make KFC!

  1. In a large bowl, marinate the chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  2. In another bowl, mix the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk, allowing excess to drip off, then dredge each piece in the flour mixture, ensuring it's well coated.
  5. Carefully place the coated chicken pieces in the hot oil, frying in batches to avoid overcrowding. Fry until golden brown and cooked through, about 12-15 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite sides like coleslaw, mashed potatoes, or biscuits.

Enjoy your homemade KFC, a dish that brings warmth and joy to every meal!
